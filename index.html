  1. Subreddits/Community Targeting

    Allowing for hyper-specific content niches. A user interested in a particular topic can join a subreddit dedicated to that subject.

    Users can also customize their front page (user profiles) by subscribing to specific subreddits.

    Targeting users based on communities they‚Äôve subscribed to. Community targeting also includes users who have viewed or visited the communities in the last 28 days, regardless of subscription status.

  1. Keyword Targeting [Beta]

    Advertisers can choose specific keywords they want to target.

    Offering different types of matches, broad or exact. For example, a broad match would mean that ads could appear for related terms or slight variations of the targeted keywords. An exact match would limit the display to instances where the exact keyword phrase is used.

    By targeting ads based on user interactions and the content they engage with, there's a higher likelihood that the ads will resonate with the user.

    *The goal of this feature is to increase the relevance of ads for users.

Potential strategies for MyVoice

1. Target Audience Profiling

Audience profiling can be generated based on three dimensions: user behavior, user needs, user pain point.

Users Behavior

  • How often users create, comment on, like, or share high-quality content [Quantifying these aspects will be crucial for in-depth analysis when shaping our user profiles.]
  • Users can choose up to 3 hashtags that best describe their content. This helps those targeting specific content to have a more tailored experience and save time
  • Topics they follow
  • How often they post reviews on movies or books
  • How regularly they participate in offline events or local meetups

User Needs

  • Contents
    • Original content generating
    • Expressing personal insights, experiences, and perspectives
    • Crafting unique posts that reflect individual creativity and voice
    • Using multimedia elements, such as images, videos, or audio, to enhance content
    • Curating lists or collections based on personal preferences or expertise
  • Sharing
    • Engaging with a Community of Like-minded Individuals
      • Participating in discussions and debates on shared interests
      • Offering feedback, suggestions, or constructive criticism on others' content
    • Using the Platform as a Space to Share Daily Moments
      • Posting updates, stories, news, or anecdotes from daily life
      • Access to unfiltered news and unbiased discussions
      • A strong appetite for uncensored content
      • Genuine, unrestricted communication
      • Sharing personal milestones, achievements, or challenges and receiving support from the community.
    • Bonding with Online Acquaintances
      • Building deeper connections through direct messaging or private interactions
      • Providing a sense of cultural and linguistic familiarity
  • Social Connections & Networking
    • Seeking Out and Befriending Intriguing Individuals
      • Discovering users with unique perspectives or backgrounds, especially for retired journalists, writers, intellectuals, and public-minded citizens who've faced restrictions on other platforms
      • Values and amplifies users‚Äô wealth of experience and insights.
      • Reestablishing their sense of purpose in a community that respects and values their vast contributions.
      • Expanding one's social circle by engaging with different user profiles
    • Building Connections with Local Users
      • Participating in local events, meetups, or gatherings organized through the platform
      • A platform that values overseas students & scholars global insights combined with local context

User Pain Points

  • Content management
    • Users joined too many groups and might get overwhelmed by the influx of new information
    • Concerned about the quality of group discussions and the presence of "shill" accounts or spam
    • Privacy and personal safety
    • Seeking a comfortable interaction experience, positive encounters with strangers, and protection of personal space.

2. Offline Grouping / Local Gathering

  • Offline Topic Integration
    • Literary and art-related topics are the most suitable for offline integration and local gathering. Currently, there are fewer "Douban Local" events, and user engagement is not significantly high comparing to online users.
    • Douban can leverage the popularity of various topics and organize offline events based on online discussions, integrating with the knowledge circulation system mentioned earlier.
    • Knowledgeable individuals with unique perspectives (journalists, social activists, scholars, authors, moviemakers, screenwriters) can be invited as guest speakers, while interested users can attend as the audience.
    • To add more commerciality to these offline events, cross-sector collaborations can be explored, such as partnerships with independent bookstores, coffee shops, live music venues, movie theaters, and more.
